About me

I am Maya Tskhovrebashvili, an artist, born in 1973. I live and work in Tbilisi, Georgia. My vision of contemporary painting is to contact the subconscious through the emotions conveyed by colors, to search for answers, to bring beauty out of the primordial chaos, and to recognize aesthetics as a feeling. I have participated in international group exhibitions of contemporary art in Europe and the USA. My works are kept in private collections in Georgia, USA, Austria, France, Norway, Germany and Sweden. Art drives the Universe and I am here to make the world better and more beautiful.
